About Me

My personal motto is: "Seek simplicity and distrust it." I like to start from simple ideas and then explore what is behind them. This way of thinking comes from my studies in philosophy, especially logic, metaphysics, and mereology (the study of parts and wholes).


Today I apply this mindset to data. I enjoy exploring datasets, breaking complex problems into smaller parts, and trying to understand the structure behind the numbers. Working with SQL, Excel, and Python, I like to transform raw data into clear insights that help people understand what is really happening.
